### 💡 About Me
I'm **Ali Chaaraoui** — a **Canadian-born architect, designer, and software developer** studying **Architecture + Computer Science** at the **University of North Carolina at Charlotte**.  

I create digital and spatial experiences that merge **technical systems** with **human emotion** — from building interactive 3D games and AI-powered dashboards to designing real-world architectural spaces.

> “Design is where logic meets emotion — I build things that live in that balance.”
